* The anti-vision is a powerful tool for clarifying one's goals and desires. 
* It involves defining what you don't want in your life, in order to create a clear picture of what you do want. 
* The anti-vision can help you identify patterns and habits that are holding you back. 
* It can also help you create a sense of direction and purpose. 
* By defining what you don't want, you can create a clear plan for achieving what you do want.
