* The P.A.R.A. method involves categorizing content into four main categories: areas, projects, resources, and archives. 
* Areas refer to ongoing aspects of your life that require maintenance. 
* Projects are specific tasks with a defined goal and deadline. 
* Resources are collections of thematic information that interest you. 
* Archives are valuable past information that you want to keep but are not currently using actively.
