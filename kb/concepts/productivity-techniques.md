* The Pomodoro Technique involves working in focused 25-minute increments, followed by a 5-minute break. 
* The 3/3/3 Method requires spending 3 hours on an important project, completing 3 shorter tasks, and doing 3 maintenance tasks. 
* The Eisenhower Matrix helps prioritize tasks based on their urgency and importance. 
* Eating the Frog involves tackling the most challenging task first. 
* The Seinfeld Strategy involves creating a chain of daily accomplishments to build momentum. 
* Time Blocking involves scheduling tasks into fixed time slots.
