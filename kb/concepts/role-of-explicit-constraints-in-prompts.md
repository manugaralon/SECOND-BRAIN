* Explicit constraints help to clarify the task and reduce ambiguity, leading to more accurate and relevant results.
* Constraints can include specific requirements, such as format, tone, or style, which help to guide the AI model's response.
* By providing explicit constraints, users can ensure that the AI model stays within the desired boundaries and produces high-quality output.
* The use of constraints can also help to reduce the risk of unwanted or off-topic responses.
* Effective constraints can be achieved through the use of specific keywords, phrases, or parameters that define the task and its requirements.
