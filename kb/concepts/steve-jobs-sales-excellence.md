summary: Steve Jobs' approach to sales excellence involves creating a unique value
  proposition, focusing on benefits, keeping the message simple, telling a compelling
  story, knowing the audience, creating an experience, not being afraid to polarize,
  and persisting in the face of rejection.
---

* Create a unique value proposition that differentiates your product or service. 
* Focus on the benefits of your product or service, rather than just its features. 
* Keep your message simple and easy to understand. 
* Tell a compelling story that resonates with your audience. 
* Know your audience inside and out, and tailor your approach accordingly. 
* Create an experience that engages and delights your customers.
